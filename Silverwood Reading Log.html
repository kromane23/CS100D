Complete the monthly reading challenge to recieve a ticket to Silverwood!

For the month of March all students are encouraged to read everyday. Students must log the books, pages, and minutes they read 5 out of 7 days a week, and submitting the form at the end of the month. Each student will receive a ticket to go to Silverwood!

Why do we celebrate Read Across America?

Books challenge, nourish and inspire. They teach important lessons. Books reveal new worlds and enrich our understanding of our own of different people, perspectives an cultures.

We kick off Read Across America on March 2, the birthday of Dr. Seuss!
