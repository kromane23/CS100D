Cinderella

or, The Little Glass Slipper

Chapter 1

There once lived a gentleman who married for his second wife the proudest woman ever seen. She had two daughters of the same spirit, who were indeed like her in all things.

No sooner was the wedding over than the stepmother began to show her ill temper; she could not bear her young step-daughter's gentle ways.
